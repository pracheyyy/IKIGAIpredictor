Philosophy

The Ikigai Framework

Four measurable pillars translate ancient Japanese wisdom into modern behavioral analytics.

What you love, what you are good at, what the world needs, and what you can be paid for.

The Ikigai framework is operationalized into four measurable behavioral pillars, allowing students to visualize balance between mental well-being and academic effort.

  • Love — Physical activity + social connection (inverted scoring to penalise overexertion)
  • Good At — Study consistency score (balanced hours score highest)
  • Need — Sleep vs stress recovery balance
  • Value — Weighted productivity output score
